About TTC DLW, Varanasi

Technical Training Centre (TTC) has been inaugurated by Shri V. V. Giri, Governor of Uttar Pradesh on 2nd Feb. 1959. It provides the training in different categories like Induction Training for newly recruited / promoted staff and supervisors, Refresher, General Management, Professional, Quality & Industrial Related Safety Courses and Skill Upgradation Courses for DLW staff.Apart from above TTC also organize internship courses for Degree and Diploma Engineering & Management students and different training for other Zonal Railways staff and supervisors.
